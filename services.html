About PrivacyBridge

Privacy risk assessments and compliance support for private healthcare clinics

Who We Are

PrivacyBridge is a privacy consulting practice focused on helping private healthcare clinics in Ontario understand and manage their obligations under PHIPA.

We work with clinics to identify privacy risks, assess data handling practices, and develop structured Privacy Impact Assessments (PIAs) that reflect how patient information is actually collected, used, stored, and shared.

What We Do

Most small clinics operate without formal privacy documentation or structured compliance processes. Our role is to bridge that gap in a practical, non-technical way.

We translate privacy requirements into clear, actionable steps so clinics can reduce risk, improve internal processes, and demonstrate due diligence if reviewed by regulators.

Why It Matters

Healthcare providers handle sensitive personal health information every day. Under PHIPA, clinics are responsible for ensuring appropriate safeguards are in place — including policies, staff training, breach response procedures, and vendor management.

Even small operational gaps can create unnecessary exposure. Our goal is to help clinics identify those gaps early and address them before they become compliance or reputational issues.
