Services

Practical PHIPA compliance support for private healthcare clinics in Ontario

What we do

We help small private clinics understand where they are exposed under PHIPA, document their data practices properly, and implement clear, practical privacy systems without unnecessary complexity.

Privacy Risk Assessment (Entry Point)

A fast, structured review of your clinic’s current privacy setup to identify immediate risk areas.

  • Patient data handling review (how information actually flows)
  • Digital systems check (email, EMR, booking tools)
  • Common PHIPA compliance gaps
  • High-level risk summary

Outcome: Clear visibility into your biggest privacy risks and what to fix first.

Privacy Impact Assessment (PIA) (Core Service)

A formal Privacy Impact Assessment documenting how patient data is collected, stored, accessed, and shared across your clinic systems.

  • End-to-end patient data flow mapping
  • Risk analysis across systems and vendors
  • PHIPA compliance gap assessment
  • Documentation suitable for audit or regulatory review

Outcome: A structured compliance foundation that demonstrates due diligence under PHIPA.

Typical engagement: Project-based (varies by clinic size and complexity)

Compliance Gap Analysis & Action Plan

A prioritized roadmap showing exactly what needs to be fixed first to reduce risk efficiently.

  • Staff training gaps
  • Policy and documentation gaps
  • Vendor and system risk review
  • Step-by-step remediation plan

Outcome: A clear execution plan from current state → compliant operations.

Staff Privacy Training

Practical training designed for clinic staff handling personal health information day-to-day.

  • PHIPA fundamentals explained simply
  • Real-world clinic scenarios (email, intake, records access)
  • Common breach prevention practices

Outcome: Reduced human error and stronger day-to-day compliance.

How we work

Every clinic is different, so we don’t use templates or generic audits. We review how your systems actually operate and build compliance around your real workflows.

The goal is simple: identify risk clearly, document properly, and reduce exposure without overcomplicating operations.
